# Random Event & Encounter Tables

Roll d20 on the relevant table when entering a new scene or after a rest. On 1-10, nothing notable happens. On 11-20, use the corresponding entry. The DM expands each seed narratively to fit the current situation.

---

## Town

| d20 | Event | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 11 | A street brawl breaks out nearby | DC 12 Perception to notice a pickpocket working the crowd |
| 12 | A merchant is selling unusual goods at a steep discount | Possible stolen goods or cursed items |
| 13 | Town crier announces news relevant to an active quest | Tie to current quest hooks |
| 14 | An NPC the player helped (or wronged) recognizes them | Use existing NPC or create a minor one |
| 15 | A patrol stops the player for questioning | DC 13 Persuasion or Deception to avoid hassle |
| 16 | A child asks for help finding something lost | Simple favor, potential reputation gain |
| 17 | Rumors at a tavern hint at a nearby opportunity | Side quest seed or lore drop |
| 18 | A faction member approaches with a proposition | Tie to faction with highest or lowest reputation |
| 19 | Something is wrong — a building on fire, a collapsed wall, screaming | Skill challenge, DC 14-16 |
| 20 | Unusual magical phenomenon — lights, whispers, a brief vision | Foreshadowing or tied to the campaign's main threat |

## Road / Travel

| d20 | Event | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 11 | A fellow traveler heading the opposite direction shares news | Information about destination or world events |
| 12 | Broken wagon blocking the road — travelers need help | DC 12 Athletics or Investigation to assist |
| 13 | Animal tracks cross the path — something large passed recently | DC 13 Survival to identify; possible encounter ahead |
| 14 | A roadside shrine, partially defaced | Religion DC 12 to identify; possible blessing or lore |
| 15 | Bandits or desperate refugees demand a toll | Social or combat encounter, CR appropriate to level |
| 16 | Weather shifts dramatically — storm, fog, or unnatural stillness | Travel complication, possible Perception checks |
| 17 | An abandoned camp with signs of a hasty departure | Investigation DC 14 reveals what happened |
| 18 | A wounded traveler or creature by the roadside | Medicine DC 12 to stabilize; story hook |
| 19 | The path splits — a shortcut through dangerous terrain | Risk/reward choice for the player |
| 20 | Corrupted terrain — dead vegetation, strange sounds, unnatural decay | Environmental hazard, CON save DC 13 |

## Wilderness

| d20 | Event | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 11 | Unusual weather — unnatural fog, blood-red sunset, static air | Atmosphere and possible foreshadowing |
| 12 | Animal encounter — not hostile, but noteworthy | Possible corruption, unusual behavior, or rare creature |
| 13 | Old ruins or a landmark not on any map | Exploration opportunity, possible loot or lore |
| 14 | A hunter's trap or territorial marking | Survival DC 13 to spot before triggering |
| 15 | Natural hazard — unstable ground, flash flood, falling rocks | DEX save DC 13 or take 1d6 damage |
| 16 | Creature encounter — hostile, CR appropriate to level | Use terrain and surprise rules |
| 17 | A hidden campsite with supplies or a cache | Investigation DC 14 to find; minor loot |
| 18 | Signs of faction activity — patrol, supply drop, ritual site | Tie to relevant faction |
| 19 | A lost or stranded NPC in need of rescue | Potential ally or information source |
| 20 | Corrupted incursion — tainted creatures or spreading supernatural decay | Combat encounter + environmental hazard |

## Dungeon

| d20 | Event | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 11 | Strange sounds echo from deeper within | Atmosphere; Perception DC 13 to identify source |
| 12 | A previous adventurer's remains with a journal entry | Lore drop or warning about ahead |
| 13 | Trap — mechanical or magical | Investigation DC 14 to spot, DEX save DC 13 to avoid |
| 14 | Environmental shift — temperature drops, water rises, lights flicker | Creates urgency or blocks a path |
| 15 | A wandering monster, separated from its group | Combat encounter, CR appropriate; possibly weakened |
| 16 | A room with signs of recent habitation | Someone else is in this dungeon |
| 17 | A puzzle or riddle guarding a minor treasure | Intelligence DC 14 or creative solution |
| 18 | Structural instability — ceiling cracks, floor gives way | DEX save DC 14 or take 2d6 damage |
| 19 | A hidden passage or secret door | Perception/Investigation DC 15 |
| 20 | A powerful creature's lair — signs of something dangerous ahead | Foreshadowing; player can choose to engage or avoid |
