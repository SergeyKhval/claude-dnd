# House Rules & Campaign Modifications

## Dice Rolling

- All dice rolls are transparent — the DM always shows the formula: `d20(natural) + modifier = total vs DC/AC -> Result`
- Critical hits: roll damage dice twice (PHB standard)
- Natural 1 on attack: automatic miss (no fumble table)
- Natural 20 on attack: automatic hit + critical damage

## Character Creation

- Ability scores: Player's choice of Standard Array, Point Buy, or 4d6 drop lowest
- Starting equipment: By class + background (PHB standard)
- HP: Max at level 1, roll or take average at subsequent levels

## Resting

- Short rest: 1 hour (PHB standard)
- Long rest: 8 hours (PHB standard)
- Long rest benefits require a safe location

## Death & Dying

- Death saving throws: PHB standard (3 successes/failures)
- Instant death: Massive damage rule applies (PHB standard)
- Character death: Player can create a new character at the same level

## Combat

- Initiative: Standard DEX-based (PHB)
- Flanking: Not used (PHB default)
- Optional: Describe creative actions for potential advantage on checks
